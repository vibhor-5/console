Kubernetes Node Management

Complete visibility into every node across your Kubernetes fleet. Monitor health conditions, track resource utilization, detect pressure events, and plan capacity from a single dashboard.

View Nodes GitHub

Infrastructure Visibility at the Node Level

Understand the health and capacity of your compute infrastructure across every cluster, provider, and region.

❤

Health Condition Tracking

Monitor Ready, MemoryPressure, DiskPressure, PIDPressure, and NetworkUnavailable conditions for every node. Color-coded indicators make it easy to spot problems instantly.

📊

Capacity Planning

Visualize allocatable vs. requested resources per node. See CPU, memory, and pod capacity to determine when clusters need additional nodes or when existing nodes are underutilized.

🔋

Resource Pressure Alerts

Detect nodes under memory, disk, or PID pressure before workloads are evicted. Historical pressure data helps identify recurring bottlenecks and inform scaling decisions.

🚫

Cordon & Drain

Safely cordon nodes for maintenance and drain workloads to healthy nodes. Track cordoned nodes across clusters and ensure no workload is left running on nodes marked for decommission.

🏷

Label & Taint Management

View and manage node labels and taints across your fleet. Filter nodes by labels, identify scheduling constraints, and ensure workload placement policies are correctly configured.

⚙

Node Pool Overview

Group nodes by pool, instance type, or availability zone. Compare resource distribution across pools and identify which node pools are under the most pressure or best suited for specific workloads.
