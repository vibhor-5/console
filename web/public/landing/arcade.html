Master Kubernetes concepts through interactive games and challenges. Schedule pods, configure network policies, troubleshoot failures, and compete on leaderboards in a safe, gamified environment embedded in your console.

Learn by Playing, Not Just Reading

Interactive challenges that build real Kubernetes skills. No sandbox cluster required. Play directly in your console and learn at your own pace.

🎮

Pod Scheduling Game

Place pods on nodes while respecting resource constraints, node affinity, taints, and tolerations. Race against the clock to schedule workloads efficiently and earn a high score.

🛡

Network Policy Puzzle

Build network policies to allow or deny traffic between services. Each level introduces new concepts: namespace isolation, port restrictions, CIDR blocks, and egress rules.

🔧

Troubleshooting Scenarios

Diagnose and fix broken deployments with only the clues a real cluster would give you. Investigate events, logs, and resource status to resolve CrashLoopBackOff, ImagePullBackOff, and more.

🏆

Achievement System

Unlock achievements as you master Kubernetes concepts. From “First Pod” to “Multi-Cluster Master,” track your progress and showcase your skills with badges on your profile.

📈

Skill Progression

Challenges are organized by difficulty and topic. Start with basic pod concepts and progress through deployments, services, RBAC, storage, and advanced scheduling as your skills grow.

👥

Team Leaderboard

Compete with colleagues and the community on global leaderboards. Track speed, accuracy, and total challenges completed. Great for team training sessions and onboarding new engineers.
